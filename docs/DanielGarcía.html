Main Tasks

Concept Art

I was in charge of making the concept art for the various environments of the game. I also made some concept art for the alien plant life that inhabits the alien planet of the game.

Animations

I was in charge of creating all animations for the Xenomorph Queen - movement, attacks, and death.

Particles
