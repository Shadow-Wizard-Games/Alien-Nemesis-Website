Our Game

Follow the story of Stub, a discharged Lieutenant being transferred to a prison colony due to her crimes. After her ship crashes on a strange planet she will have to adapt and survive the adverse conditions and try to escape.

"Alien: Nemesis is a fast paced shooter with exploration, in a dark environment plagued by aliens that will not hesitate to attack if they need to. Developed by Shadow Wizard Games Studios, it is based on the videogame series Diablo, created by Blizzard, and with its last release being in 2023. The aesthetic of the game is based on the Alien franchise started in 1978."

Game Features

Alien Franchise

The game's theme is based on the original movies of the Alien saga.

Game Mechanics

"Diablo" like game mechanics where top-down action fights prevail.

Low Poly Art Style

Retro looking aesthetic accomplished by using this 3D Modeling style.

Variety of Enemies

Different enemies throughout the game based on the Alien franchise.

Own Game Engine

The game is fully developed using our own game engine, TheOneEngine.
