Shadow Wizard Money Games is a group of 23 students who have come together to create our game Alien: Nemesis. We are studying in the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC), finishing our third year of studies of the bachelor’s degree of Video Game Design and Development. Our goal is to complete the subject Project III as if we were a small indie studio developing a game with our own engine.
